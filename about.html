As a child I yearned for the mines.

The phrase "As a child, I yearned for the mines" has become a notable meme, often used to humorously reference children's enthusiasm for mining activities, particularly in the context of the video game Minecraft. This meme gained popularity through social media platforms and has been featured in various online discussions and content.

The origin of this meme can be traced back to a 2022 tweet by user @Froggenthusias1, stating, "Minecraft proves that abolishing child labour was a mistake. The children yearn for the mines." This tweet satirically commented on the game's mining mechanics and sparked widespread sharing and adaptation across the internet. The phrase has since been used to humorously critique or highlight discussions around child labor laws, especially in light of legislative changes in certain U.S. states.

In popular culture, the meme has been referenced in various media, including a Minecraft movie trailer, where the line is delivered in a dramatic fashion, further cementing its place in internet humor.
