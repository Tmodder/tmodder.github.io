Jack Black's "Steve's Lava Chicken" Breaks Chart Records

Jack Black's latest musical venture, "Steve's Lava Chicken," from A Minecraft Movie, has taken the entertainment world by storm. This 34-second novelty track has not only captivated audiences but also made history by becoming the shortest song ever to enter the Billboard Hot 100, debuting at No. 78 for the week of May 3, 2025.

A Sizzling Scene Steals the Show

In A Minecraft Movie, Black voices the protagonist, Steve, who concocts a fiery method of cooking chicken using lava. This humorous scene is accompanied by the catchy tune "Steve's Lava Chicken," co-written by Black and director Jared Hess. The song's playful lyrics and sound effects, including sizzling lava and clucking chickens, have resonated with fans worldwide.

Chart-Topping Achievements

Beyond its Billboard success, "Steve's Lava Chicken" has also made waves internationally. In the UK, it peaked at number nine on the UK Singles Chart, breaking the record for the shortest song to ever appear in the top 40. The song's viral popularity underscores the cultural impact of A Minecraft Movie, which has grossed over $816 million globally as of April 27, 2025.

From Screen to Meme

The infectious nature of "Steve's Lava Chicken" has transcended the screen, sparking a multitude of memes and social media trends. Fans have embraced the song's quirky charm, creating dance challenges and parody videos that have further propelled its popularity.

A Legacy of Musical Humor

Jack Black's foray into musical comedy is not new. His previous hit, "Peaches," from The Super Mario Bros. Movie, also garnered significant attention. With "Steve's Lava Chicken," Black continues to blend humor and music, solidifying his reputation as a versatile entertainer.

Experience the Phenomenon

For those eager to experience the viral sensation, the official music video for "Steve's Lava Chicken" is available on YouTube. Additionally, fans can stream the song on major music platforms and enjoy the extended version featured on the EP I...Am Steve.
